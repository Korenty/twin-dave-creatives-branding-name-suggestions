Identity Analysis

Bridging the gap between current market perception and future strategic goals.

● The Current Landscape

  • Perception: Reliable local tradesman, functional, "gets the job done."
  • Limitations: Name lacks scalability; blends in with general contractors; doesn't communicate specialized finishing expertise.
  • Visual Tone: Utilitarian, undistinguished in a competitive market.

★ The Future Beacon

  • Goal Perception: Premium finishing authority, meticulous detail, architectural partner.
  • Requirement: A name that sounds engineered, high-value, and capable of handling complex commercial projects.
  • Visual Tone: Sleek, industrial-chic, disciplined, and sophisticated.
